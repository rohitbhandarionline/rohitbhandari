About Rohit


Rohit Bhandari is a data-driven storyteller and AI strategist known for translating the most complex ideas in artificial intelligence, data, and automation into practical, powerful tools for everyday people. He’s spent the past 6+ years building real-world data solutions—from optimizing supply chains for enterprises to upskilling thousands of aspiring data professionals through YouTube, live coding streams, and online content. With a mission to make AI more human and accessible, Rohit brings together technical depth, business clarity, and inspirational storytelling. From working as a full-stack developer to becoming a data engineer and now an educator, his career arc reflects the kind of transformation he helps others achieve: breaking past career plateaus, making smarter choices, and thriving in an AI-first world. Growing online presence, Rohit has empowered working professionals, students, and solopreneurs to leverage tools like ChatGPT, Python, SQL, and Azure without overwhelm. His work blends human psychology, business efficiency, and tech strategy—making him a relatable guide for anyone navigating the future of work.
